Shipping Policy

Last updated: April 2024

1. Shipping Locations

We ship all across India. International shipping is currently not available but we are working on it!

2. Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days after payment confirmation.

3. Delivery Estimates

Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days. Express delivery (if available) takes 2-4 business days.

4. Shipping Charges

We offer free standard shipping on all orders above ₹999. For orders below this amount, a flat shipping fee of ₹99 applies.
